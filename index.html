About Me

I am a master’s student at TU Chemnitz specializing in data science, with a strong background in mathematics that shapes my analytical and structured approach to problem-solving. Through my studies, I have built expertise in optimization, machine learning, statistical modeling, and real-world data analysis. I enjoy uncovering patterns in complex data and turning them into meaningful insights.

My journey has also strengthened my resilience and adaptability, which shape how I learn and solve problems. I am especially interested in using data science to understand complex systems and build practical, impactful solutions that support better decisions in everyday life.
